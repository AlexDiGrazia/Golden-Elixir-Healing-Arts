Golden Elixir Healing Arts

Release, Relax, Heal

A place where healing happens. . .

Hi! My name is Alex and I help people get out of pain.

My speciality is in gentle Osteopathic techniques that restore balance to the body by facilitiating release in a connective tissue called Fascia that surrounds every muscle, bone, and organ in the human structure.

The primary focus of my model is not only providing treatment, but sharing the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary for clients to take the healing experience home with them - with the ultimate goal of client self-sufficiency and Me becoming an obsolete part of their process.

Treatment begins with a thorough constultation where we discuss goals, strategy, and potential self-healing options to go over at the end of session.

The entirety of Golden Elixir Healing is an extension of my own personal practice. The skills, mindset, and resources are what I use personally to stay limber, pain-free, and poised to meet the challenges of life.
